Overview

Welcome to the Global Shapers Tshwane Hub, where passionate young leaders come together to shape a better future. As part of a global community initiated by the World Economic Forum, we are dedicated to driving positive change through impactful local projects and initiatives.

Our hub serves as a platform for youth to connect, collaborate, and take action on key issues affecting our community, including education, sustainability, and economic empowerment. With a focus on innovation and inclusivity, we work tirelessly to empower individuals and uplift communities in Tshwane and beyond.

Join us in our mission to create lasting impact and inspire the next generation of changemakers. Together, we can make a difference!
