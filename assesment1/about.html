About Global Shapers Tshwane Hub

Overview

The Global Shapers Tshwane Hub is a group of young individuals who are committed to shaping a better future for their community. The Hub is a platform for young people to engage in social impact projects, build leadership skills, and create lasting change.

Mission

Our mission is to empower young people in Tshwane by creating opportunities for them to be actively involved in solving pressing local challenges, developing their skills, and leading initiatives that benefit their communities.

Vision

We envision a future where young leaders from Tshwane are driving innovation and sustainable development in their communities, with a strong network of global partners supporting their growth.

Our Values

  • Leadership: Empowering young people to lead by example.
  • Collaboration: Working together to amplify social impact.
  • Innovation: Using creative solutions to tackle community challenges.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards in everything we do.
  • Impact: Creating lasting change in the lives of others.
